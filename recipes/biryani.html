Biryani

Description

Make a fragrant Chicken Dum Biryani by marinating chicken in yogurt and spices. Layer it with parboiled Basmati rice, saffron milk, and fried onions, then steam slowly (dum) until tender. Prep takes 30 minutes, cooking takes 50 minutes.

Ingredients

For the Chicken Marinade:

  • 1 kg bone-in chicken
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt (curd)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tbsp Biryani Masala
  • Salt to taste

For the Rice and Layering

  • 3 cups aged Basmati rice (soaked for 30 mins)
  • 3 tbsp ghee
  • 1/2 cup fried onions (birista)
  • A pinch of saffron soaked in 3 tbsp warm milk
  • Whole spices (cloves, cardamom, cinnamon, bay leaves)
  • Chopped mint and coriander leaves

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Marinate: In a large bowl, mix all the marinade ingredients with the chicken. Let it rest for at least 1 hour in the fridge.
  2. Boil Rice: In a large pot, bring 4-5 liters of water to a boil with salt and the whole spices. Add the soaked rice and cook until it is 70-80% done (it should have a slight bite). Drain and set aside.
  3. Cook Chicken: Heat oil and a little ghee in a heavy-bottomed pot. Add the marinated chicken and cook on medium heat until the chicken is tender and the oil separates.
  4. Layer: Remove some of the cooked chicken to keep the layer flat. Top the chicken with a layer of the boiled rice, fried onions, mint, and coriander. Add another layer of chicken and top with the remaining rice, saffron milk, and ghee.
  5. Dum (Steam): Cover the pot tightly with aluminum foil and place the heavy lid on top. Cook on the lowest possible heat for 20 minutes. Let it rest for 15 minutes before opening.
